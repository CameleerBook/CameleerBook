About Us

What?

"Apprendre la vérification avec Cameleer" is a textbook for learning formal verification of software through the use of automated deductive tools, such as Cameleer, Why3 and SMT solvers.

Why?

In various contexts software failure can lead to serious consequences, for instance endangering human lives. Verifying correctness through unit testing is insufficient, as it cannot guarantee the absence of bugs. Instead, we must rely on formal methods and their teaching.

How?

"Apprendre la vérification avec Cameleer" uses classical algorithms and data structures to teach about verification. By using well-known problems, we believe most readers will be vaguely familiarized or, at the very least, can be easily search on other sources of information.
